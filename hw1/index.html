Task 1

Walk through how you rasterize triangles in your own words.

Here's how I tackle triangle rasterization: I start by identifying the triangle's bounding box based on the max and min x and y values. Then, I loop through every pixel inside this box to check if it falls within the triangle. We consider both counter-clockwise and clockwise triangle windings, using a point-in-triangle (three-line) test for each. If a pixel passes the test in either scenario, it's inside the triangle, and we go ahead and rasterize it.
